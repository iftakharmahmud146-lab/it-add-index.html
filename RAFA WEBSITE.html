RAFA RESOURCES PROFILE

A registered international education consultancy based in Malaysia, specializing in guiding students from South Asia, the Middle East, and East Asia to pursue higher education opportunities in Malaysia.

Company Introduction

RAFA Resources is a registered international education consultancy based in Malaysia. We specialize in guiding students from South Asia, the Middle East, and East Asia to pursue higher education opportunities in Malaysia.

We are a Bangladeshi-based education and visa consultancy agency, dedicated to building a strong bridge between Malaysian universities and Bangladeshi students.

With trusted partnerships, professional counseling, and end-to-end student services, RAFa Resources connects ambition with opportunity.

Our Mission

To deliver trusted, transparent, and student-centered recruitment services that simplify the path from ambition to achievement.

Our Vision

To empower students with the opportunity to achieve international education dreams, building futures without borders.

Our vision is to become a trusted bridge between Bangladeshi students and Malaysian universities.

Our Goals

  • Expand student recruitment in South Asia, Middle East, and East Asia
  • Build long-term university partnerships
  • Promote Malaysia as a premier higher education destination
